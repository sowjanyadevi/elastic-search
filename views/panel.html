Employee Referral Program

Why SkilledTree is best for employee referral?

SkilledTree is an Automated Multi Level employee referral program. It allows the employees of any organization to add their referral friends who are looking for job. This will maximize the job reach and candidates will be selected for company’s cultural fit.

SkilledTree encourages the employees of an organization to refer the suitable candidates from their social networks. As a reward, the employer pays the referral employee.

To motivate active participation, set up gamification to reward employees with points for certain activities and hence, gamification is optional

Social Media too will become the driving force behind Employee Referral. Candidates looking for jobs will seek actively expand their online networks in order to gain access to employees in their target organizations

Tracking Reports and Analysis are part of SkilledTree. All the reports can be exported in the desired form

You'll save yourself time and money by using fewer recruitment sources. And by hiring people who already get along, you'll have a head start on building good team.

Overview

Companies

If you have the company size of more than 200+ employees then SkilledTree is the best option for your employee referral program. Your company account will be created in SkilledTree. Maximum job reach claims the benefits to be an improved candidate quality fit. Candidate 'fit' to the company's culture and teams.

Employees

Employees in your organization are the soul of the referral program. People who already work for you will tend to refer people with work ethics similar to their own. If you have high-quality employees, you will likely receive referrals of the same,heading towards an efficient recruitment process.

Referrals

Employees who are hired through referrals tend to stay with the company longer than other candidates. So get your dignified employees by encouraging your current employees to add more referrals to the team.

Team

What is Team?


Company can have multiple teams. Each team can have multiple employees. Employees under one team can be moved into another team.



Team wise notifications


These employees will receive the jobs notifications which are posted in their team network. Job Notifications can be notified by SMS, EMail and via GCM.

Regions

If the company has various branches across the world, each team has been grouped into multiple regions. Each region can have multiple cities.

Cities

If the company has various regions, multiple cities will be grouped under each regions. Each city can have multiple teams.

Roles

HR Head

Hr Head is the person who is devised to administer the employee referral program on regional-wise. A company can have only one HR Head.



Regional manager

HR Regional Manager is the person who is devised to manage the HR Managers under each regions.



HR Manager

HR Manager will have all the authorities of recruitment process. He can post a job, checklist the referral resumes and select the suitable candidate ‘fit’ for the company within his concerned city where he belongs to.



Recruiter

Recruiter is devised to manage the interview process for assigned referral candidates. He can post a referral job in his wall and that will be reachable to his entire team.
